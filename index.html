About

This farm between edinburgh and carlisle was like any other farm between the Anglo-Scottish
border. until one day the owner of the farm decided that he was bored and wanted some montey and so converted
the farm into a visitor attraction with a shop, a small petting zoo, a cafe and a playground.

Cafe

This farm may be an attraction for visiters from the anglo-scottish border but that
does not mean they can't replenish themselfes, thats where cafes come in and it has food and drinks from across
the four countries of the uk, like irn-bru or a Full breakfast.
